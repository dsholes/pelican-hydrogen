Alpha Centauri

Date: January 01, 2001

The sun's closest stellar neighbors are three stars in the Alpha Centauri system. The two main stars are Alpha Centauri A and Alpha Centauri B, which form a binary pair. The third star is Proxima Centauri. (Read More)
